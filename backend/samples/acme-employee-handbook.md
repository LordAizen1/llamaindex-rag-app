# Acme Robotics — Employee Handbook

## Overview

Acme Robotics is a manufacturer of warehouse automation systems founded in 2011
and headquartered in Portland, Oregon. This handbook summarizes the policies
that apply to all full-time and part-time employees.

## Paid Time Off

Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year. PTO begins
accruing on the first day of employment at a rate of 1.67 days per month.
Unused PTO of up to 5 days may be carried over into the following calendar year;
any balance above 5 days is forfeited on December 31.

Part-time employees who work at least 20 hours per week accrue PTO on a prorated
basis. Sick leave is separate from PTO and is capped at 10 days per year.

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements require Director-level approval and are reviewed every
6 months. Remote employees must be reachable during the core collaboration hours
of 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM Pacific Time.

## Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses under $75 do not require a receipt. Expenses of $75 or more
must be submitted with an itemized receipt within 30 days through the Concur
portal. Reimbursements are paid on the next scheduled payroll run after approval.

## Security and Devices

All company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled and screen lock set
to 5 minutes. Employees must complete the annual security awareness training by
March 31 each year. Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT Security within
24 hours.

## Parental Leave

Acme offers 16 weeks of paid parental leave to the primary caregiver and 6 weeks
to the secondary caregiver. Leave must be taken within 12 months of the birth or
adoption of a child.
